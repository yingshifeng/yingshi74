Artist Statement

As an artist, I focus on exploring the relationship between human and nature. I am the person who enjoys nature and has inspired by environment a lot. But according to the time pass and human activities. Many issues come up, and our planet becomes worst. I am trying to address those problems in my work.
In the art piece "Our future," As more plastic is produced and discarded, this will affect oceanic pollution. I search the image of pollution of the sea，then put them together to make an island. Now is a small island, but if we do not stop this, it will become the whole world. In my other art piece "Ocean," I use this idea again and create a website, also combine the P5js technic as well. it can interact with the viewer. Compare to the “Our future”.I think it's a better way to push the idea, to remind people how human behavior impacts other life on this planet.
